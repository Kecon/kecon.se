Online Base64 Encoder

This service helps you to Base64 encode online. The conversion is locally processed in your browser to ensure your privacy.

The text is encoded as UTF-8 which is the most common encoding on the web and supports all languages.

More information regarding Base64 is found at Wikipedia.
